//Courier Profile & Availability
registerCourier: Registers a new courier.
updateCourierProfile: Updates courier profile details.
getCourierProfile: Retrieves courier profile details.
deleteCourierAccount: Deletes a courier’s account.
deleteCourierXAccount: Deletes a courier’s account.
setCourierAvailability: Marks the courier as available/unavailable for delivery.
//Order & Delivery Management
viewAssignedOrders: Retrieves all orders currently assigned to the courier.
acceptOrderAssignment: Accepts a courier assignment for an order.
rejectOrderAssignment: Rejects an order assignment.
markOrderPickedUp: Marks an order as picked up from the vendor.
markOrderDelivered: Marks an order as successfully delivered.
updateOrderStatus: Updates the status of a delivery in progress.
//Navigation & Tracking
getDeliveryRoute: Retrieves the optimal route from the vendor to the customer.
updateCourierLocation: Updates the courier’s real-time location.
trackCourierLocation: Retrieves a courier’s current location (for customers/vendors).
//Earnings & Analytics
getEarningsReport: Retrieves courier earnings over a specified period.
getOrderDeliveryStats: Analyzes delivery times and success rates.
getCustomerRatings: Retrieves customer ratings and feedback for the courier.
//Notifications & Communication
viewCourierNotifications: Retrieves notifications related to new orders and system messages.
sendStatusUpdateNotification: Sends a status update notification to vendors/customers.
